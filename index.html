Spider-man
The popular arachnid-based and justice-seeking vigilante.










Virgin cover of Web of Spider-Man #129.1 (October 2012) by Mike McKone and Morry Hollowell

Nowadays, there are very few people who do not know of Spider-man or have not at least heard of him in passing. He represents peace and justice and is a role model for many young children. Additionally, he is commonly portrayed as a teenager in high school, which explains his popularity amongst people in that age group.
