Spider-man
The popular arachnid-based and justice-seeking vigilante.
















Spider-Man's spider-powers include his superhuman strength; Spider-Man has the proportionate strength of a spider, which allows him to lift 10 tons or more as if he is enraged or under extreme stress.

Spider-Man possesses his superhuman speed; Spider-Man has the proportionate speed of a spider, allowing him to run at speeds faster than a normal human being, cars, and airplanes, and it also allows him to dodge the attacks and bullets with his signature spider-sense, agility, and reflexes during combat.

Spider-Man possesses superhuman agility; Spider-Man has the proportionate agility of a spider and with the mix of his speed and reflexes, he is talented at dodging enemies' attacks or any weaponry attack.

Spider-Man also possesses superhuman jump, which allows him to jump and leap at 100 m or higher and to jump from building to building.

Spider-Man also possesses superhuman reflexes in combination with his sense, allowing him to dodge any attack, including gunfire, and to react quickly before anything can happen so that he can be able to dodge or counter the attack.

Spider-Man's superhuman stamina enables him to be more active without feeling tired or in a state of exhaustion and capable of holding his breath for minutes at a time, among other things and thus, he can endure almost anything.

Spider-Man possesses superhuman equilibrium which grants him coordination and balance, allowing him to adjust his position by sense on any object, despite how small or narrow it is.

Spider-Man's best-known spider-power, is his precognitive sixth sense referred to as his "spider-sense", which alerts him to danger. Because of this he is described as "untouchable" in combat.
